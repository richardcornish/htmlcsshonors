Assessment

Students in the Spectrum program are assessed because the course is a Credit Intensive Honors course, which makes it eligible for high school credit.

Students in the Apogee program are not assessed.

Pre-assessment

Students will receive a pre-assessment “quiz” that will determine how much existing knowledge they have about the course topics. Students will receive full quiz credit for merely taking it.

Post-assessment

Students will receive a post-assessment quiz identical to the pre-assessment quiz to determine how much students learned from the course. The post-assessment quiz will include additional questions seeking feedback about the performance of the instructor and teaching assistant.
