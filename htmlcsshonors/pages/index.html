HTML & CSS Honors

HTML & CSS Honors is the website for the “HTML & CSS Honors” and “Web Design: Introduction to HTML & CSS” summer courses that teach academically talented students how to create websites in the computer languages of HTML and CSS.

Both courses are part of the Spectrum and Apogee summer programs for gifted students offered by the Center for Talent Development, a non-profit center within the School of Education and Social Policy at Northwestern University.
