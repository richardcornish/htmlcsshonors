HTML & CSS Honors

HTML & CSS Honors is the website for the “HTML & CSS Honors” summer course that teaches academically talented students how to create websites in the computer languages of HTML and CSS.

The course is part of the Spectrum summer program for gifted students offered by the Center for Talent Development, a non-profit center within the School of Education and Social Policy at Northwestern University.

A big thank you to all the students who took the HTML & CSS Honors course this year. I encourage students to continue their coding journeys at websites like Codecademy, Khan Academy, and Code School. Programming languages such as JavaScript, Python, Ruby, Java, Swift, and C++ are all solid choices. Enjoy your summers! — </keep coding>, Rich
