HTML & CSS Honors

HTML & CSS Honors is a summer course that teaches academically talented students how to create websites in the computer languages of HTML and CSS.

The course is one session of the Spectrum and Apogee summer programs for gifted students offered by the Center for Talent Development, a non-profit center within the School of Education and Social Policy at Northwestern University.
